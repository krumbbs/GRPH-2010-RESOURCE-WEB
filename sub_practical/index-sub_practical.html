Practical

Under “Practical” you can find information about new things that you can try and apply to what you’re already working with in your own life, primarily through my own experiences and practices. I’ll touch on different applications/resources like Obsidian, cheap(er) places to purchase items that might help in your time management, like Walmart, and the intricacies of media, preferences and all. Use these writings to explore how to efficiently, effectively, and comfortably work within the tools that you have with a focus on making sure what you choose to do stays as accessible to you (physically, financially, et cetera) as possible.

Learning about certain free applications and tools that make planning easier, how to plan in group settings
How to “make due” with what you have, when it might be time to purchase something, the differences between community-made and corporately-enveloped marketplaces
The importance of “tactile feel”, how to explore your tactile preferences, what to look out for while finding your “tactile feel”
