Informational

Within “Informational” you can expect to touch on primarily theory—topics like “biological prime time”, time management techniques such as time blocking, or even a partial book analysis meant to help you reflect on your own mindset—as well as how to apply what you learn into practice. You’ll find that a lot the topics in this section are very low-agency; it’ll be up to you to use the information and reflective thoughts I give you to apply what you learn into your own life. This website is built to help you, not scare you—any examples of my own habits is my attempt at giving you ideas on what you might want to look into or apply to yourself.
